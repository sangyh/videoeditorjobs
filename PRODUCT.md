# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Video editors looking for credible, relevant work and serious creator teams looking for editors who fit a recurring production workflow. The initial buyer wedge is coaches, consultants, educators, founders, podcasters, and creator-led businesses that already publish video and need dependable long-form-to-short-form output.

## Product Purpose

Video Editor Jobs turns a fragmented hiring market into a focused path from clear workflow to matched proof. It helps editors find source-attributed opportunities, helps hiring teams describe the work precisely, and makes portfolio fit, turnaround, software, review cadence, and recurring capacity visible before either side invests in a paid test.

## Brand Personality

Calm, human, selective. The site should feel like a well-run creative studio: visually literate, specific about the work, and confident enough to avoid hype.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic freelancer marketplace, a dense corporate job board, a neon editing-software dashboard, or a long document made from repeated bordered cards. Avoid stock SaaS gradients, glassmorphism, oversized empty heroes, editorial-serif affectation, and visual noise that competes with the jobs.

## Design Principles

- Put real opportunities and clear next steps in the first viewport.
- Show taste through a small number of decisive moving-image stills, not decorative collage.
- Make workflow fit easier to scan than generic role categories.
- Keep the two-sided market legible without repeating the employer call to action.
- Use calm authority to build trust; specificity should carry more weight than hype.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard access. Preserve visible focus states, semantic headings, descriptive image alternatives, 44-pixel touch targets where practical, and a complete experience for reduced-motion users. Never rely on color alone to communicate role fit, availability, or interaction state.
